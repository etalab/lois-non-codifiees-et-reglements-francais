# Article 1

La lutte contre les virus Tomato yellow leaf curl begomovirus (TYLCV), Cucurbit yellow stunting disorder crinivirus (CYSDV), Tomato chlorosis crinivirus (ToCV), Tomato infectious chlorosis crinivirus (TICV) et Cucumber vein yellowing ipomovirus (CVYV) est obligatoire sur tout le territoire national.
